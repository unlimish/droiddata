<p><i>P2Play</i> is an Android Application for Peertube. Features include:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Show recent, popular and local list of videos.
  <li>Reproduce videos (very simple)
  <li>Login and register in your instance
  <li>Pull to refresh
  <li>Show uploaded videos
  <li>Subscribe to accounts
  <li>Show your subscripcion videos
  <li>Rate videos
</ul>
