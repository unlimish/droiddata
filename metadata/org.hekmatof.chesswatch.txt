Auto Name:ChessWatch
Summary:Simple chess clock
Description:
Clock for games like chess. It uses [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_control Fischer-After time control] method.
To use, you should provide time for each player as initial time and
delay time. After each move the delay time will be added to the
remaining time of the player.
