Description:
MPD remote control client that is optimized for speed (even with large music
libraries).

It strictly follows the Material design guidelines.

It's main features are a fast music library browser (artist, album, file
browser, playlist management).

It also includes an launcher widget and an optional notification with a
background service.

Artwork download available via MusicBrainz, Fanart.TV and Last.fm.

Works from MPD v. 0.14 and newer
.
