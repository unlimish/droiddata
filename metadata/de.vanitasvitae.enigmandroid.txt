Summary:Simulation of the Enigma Machine
Description:
This is a simulation of the famous Enigma cipher machine used in the
twentieth century and especially in the Second World War to encipher
and decipher secret messages.

It has been broken by the great british mathematician Alan Turing.

Features:

* Authentic Rotors
* Ringsettings
* Double Step Anomaly

More Information about the historical Enigma can be found on
[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enigma_machine Wikipedia].
