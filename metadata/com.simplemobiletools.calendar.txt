Auto Name:Calendar
Summary:A simple calendar with events and a customizable widget
Description:
A monthly calendar without any other calendar integration. You can easily create
events and setup reminders.

Contains a resizable 4x4 widget where you can customize the color of the text,
as well as the alpha and the color of the background. To open the app from the
widget press the month label.

This app contains no ads or unnecessary permissions. It is fully opensource,
provides a Dark theme too.
