Summary:Messaging app
Description:
Replacement for the stock AOSP messaging app.

Some of its features include:

* Material Design
* Themes
* Night Mode
* Reply to messages without leaving the current app
* Group Messaging
* MMS
* Delayed message sending
* Search
.
