Name:MidniteSolar Classic Monitor
Auto Name:Classic
Summary:View solar charging device status
Description:
Unofficial status monitor for [http://www.midnitesolar.com/ Midnite solar]'s
Classic 150, 200, 250 Charge Controller. It is a Read Only Program, it does
not write to the Classic.

If the app detects a Whizbang Junior current monitor, the State Of Charge
will display along with a bi-directional current gauge. Basic support for
the Tristar MPPT charge controller from Morningstar is also included.
.
