Auto Name:TwelF
Summary:Theme for CM12 devices using only FLOSS apps
Description:
TwelF is a Theme based on CM12 Theme Engine. Your ROM must include the NEW CM12 Theme Engine!.

This theme contains exclusively icons for the apps hosted on F-droid FLOSS Repository. More icons, bootanimation, ringtones, wallpapers... avaliable on next updates.

TwelF follows the guidelines of Google Material Design for Android Lollipop (Android 5) aiming to provide a unified and minimalistic look at devices using CM12 with only Libre/Open Apps.
