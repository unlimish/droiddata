Summary:Simple brick breaker game
Description:
Simple brick breaker game with a retro style, e.g. no textures but plain
colors and simple animations. It features sound effects for all possible
game events, a score multiplier and four brick types: Normal (white),
explive (red), strong (gray) and moving bricks (green). Levels are random
generated and three difficult settings are available.
