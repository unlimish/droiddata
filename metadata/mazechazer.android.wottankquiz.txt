Summary: A quiz app about the PC game World of Tanks
Description:
The WoT Tank Quiz is about guessing the names of tanks from the PC game World of Tanks from their images.
For each picture there are always 4 differnt possible answers.
Depeding on the level of difficulty, these options will be rather different or very similar to each other.
