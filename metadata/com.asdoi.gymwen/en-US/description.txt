Official app for the [http://gym-wen.de/startseite/ Gymnasium Wendelstein].

With this app pupils are able to:
* check their teachers substitutions
* search through the teachers list
* book food in the local cafeteria
* use mebis
* and much more

For more information about the [https://gitlab.com/asdoi Developer] you can visit [https://asdoi.gitlab.io/ this site].
