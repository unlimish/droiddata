Description:
An android signal generator with twirly knob.

* Frequency range 10Hz - 25KHz
* Level range 0dB - -80dB

The frequency knob responds to finger twirling. The fine frequency and level
sliders allow for fine adjustments of frequency and output level. The frequency
knob is also adjustable using the left and right arrow buttons.
