Summary:Notes management for ownCloud
Description:
Use "My Own Notes" to create, edit, delete and sync your notes with your ownCloud. "My Own Notes" does not send user's data anywhere else but his configured ownCloud server.

Please be aware you need:
- ownCloud Notes installed server wise: [https://github.com/owncloud/notes]
- a valid SSL certificate: See [https://blog.entwicklerbier.org/2014/05/securing-the-internet-of-things-how-about-securing-the-internet-first/ why] and [https://blog.entwicklerbier.org/2014/06/self-signed-ssl-on-speed/ how]
