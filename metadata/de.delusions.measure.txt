Summary:Weight tracker
Description:
Droid Weight is a simple, easy to use, weight tracking
app that helps you keep a journal of your weight on your
device. Besides weight, allows to track few other body parameters.
Calculates BMI (Body Mass Index). Draws graphs of parameters across
the time. Allows to enter a value just for current date/time (not
what measured yesterday and forgot to add), but supports CSV
import/export.
