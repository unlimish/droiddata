Auto Name:Music Player
Summary:A clean music player with a customizable widget
Description:
A music player easily controllable from the status bar, home screen widget or by
hardware buttons on your headset. You can sort the tracks by the Title or
Artist, as well as change the Equalizer. Does not yet support filtering by
artist or album, stay tuned!

It contains a widget with customizable text color, alpha and the color of the
background.

Contains no ads or unnecessary permissions. It is fully opensource, provides a
Dark theme too.
