<p>WECHANGE is the cooperative online collaboration platform for all who will shape the future together. More than 35,000 people are connected on <a href='https://wechange.de/'>wechange.de</a>, working together on online documents, coordinate appointments, manage shared files, and more.</p>
<p>WECHANGE is Open Source, meets highest data protection requirements, and is hosted on servers in Germany, driven by 100% CO2 neutral eco power. The WECHANGE communityware is also deployed as white-label solution for organisations, cities and communities.</p>
